VOICE PERSONA EVALUATION

Standalone acoustic scoring system using librosa, numpy, and pytest to validate AI agent voice personas. Measures MOS scoring, pitch/jitter/shimmer, tone consistency, and breakpoint detection across four configurable judge personas: empathetic, professional, efficient, and friendly.
